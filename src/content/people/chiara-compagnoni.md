Chiara Compagnoni received the B.Sc. degree in Communication Design in 2023, and the M.Sc. degree (cum laude) in Digital and Interaction Design in 2025, both from Politecnico di Milano, Italy. She expanded her academic background through international mobility programs at LAB University of Applied Sciences in Finland and NTNU in Norway. She is currently a Research Fellow at the University of Pavia, Pavia, Italy, and a member of RAMSLAB. Her research interests include interaction design, user-centered design, and real-time simulation, with applications in virtual reality (VR), human-machine interfaces (HMI), and immersive responsive environments. She has contributed to several innovative projects focused on interactive systems, serious games for cognitive training, and sustainable mobility interfaces.

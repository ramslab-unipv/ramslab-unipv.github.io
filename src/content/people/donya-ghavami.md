Donya Ghavami received the B.Sc. degree in Industrial Engineering from the Islamic Azad University, Karaj Branch, Iran, in 2019, and the M.Sc. degree in Industrial Automation Engineering from the University of Pavia, Pavia, Italy, in 2025. She is currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree in Electronics, Computer Science and Electrical Engineering at the University of Pavia, where her research focuses on robotic technologies for industrial manufacturing and pharmaceutical production environments. Her research interests include industrial robotics, automation, computer vision, biomedical manufacturing, and robotic systems for pharmaceutical and tissue engineering applications. She has been involved in several international research projects, including MED4REPAIR and TEN4CARE, focused on advanced medical devices and regenerative medicine. Her recent research activities include experimental validation of robotic systems, image-based analysis, and automation technologies for controlled pharmaceutical environments.

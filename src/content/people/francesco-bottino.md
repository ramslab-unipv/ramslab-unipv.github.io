Francesco Bottino obtained a Bachelor degree in Computer Engineering at Politecnico di Milano, Milano, Italy, in 2021, and the M.Sc. degree in Cinema and Media Engineering at Politecnico di Torino, Torino, Italy, in 2024. He is currently a PhD candidate in Electronics, Computer Science and Electrical Engineering at University of Pavia, Pavia, Italy, and a member of RAMSLAB, under tutor Prof. Hermes Giberti. His works inquires the Virtual Reality experience through simulators, focusing on factors that affect the immersiveness and virtual motion sickness. His interests then include virtual environment design, game engines and human-machine interaction. He authored publications in international journals and conferences.

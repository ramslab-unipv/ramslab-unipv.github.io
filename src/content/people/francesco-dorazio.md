Francesco D'Orazio is a PhD Candidate at RAMSLAB.

His research focuses on the robotic manufacturing of composite components. In particular, he deals with advanced process planning techniques, such as robotic filament winding and automated fiber placement. His work involves complex path planning on 3D meshes and the control of high-degree-of-freedom robotic cells.

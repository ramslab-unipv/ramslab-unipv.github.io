Michele Bedodi earned his B.Sc. in Mechanical Engineering in 2023 and his M.Sc. (cum laude) in the same field in 2025, both from Politecnico di Milano in Milan, Italy. Since September 2024, he has been conducting research at RAMSLAB within the Department of Industrial and Information Engineering at the University of Pavia, Italy, under the supervision of Prof. Nicola Giulietti and Prof. Hermes Giberti. In April 2026, he began his Ph.D. studies at the same laboratory. His research interests encompass computer vision-based measurement systems, advanced image processing for flow diagnostics, and data-driven models applied to robotic control and parameter optimization. Additionally, he has co-authored a publication in an international scientific journal focusing on vision-based automated counting systems.

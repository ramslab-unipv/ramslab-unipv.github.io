Nicola Giulietti received the M.Sc. degree cum laude in Mechanical Engineering from the Universita Politecnica delle Marche, Ancona, Italy, in 2018, and the Ph.D. degree in Industrial Engineering from the same institution in 2022. He is currently an Assistant Professor with the Department of Electronics, Computer Science, and Electrical Engineering, University of Pavia, Pavia, Italy, and a member of RAMSLAB. He teaches the course "Sensors and Computer Vision Systems." His research interests include measurement systems based on computer vision, artificial intelligence, and data-driven models, with applications in mechanical and thermal measurement system, structural health monitoring, and industrial process control. He has been involved in several European projects under the Horizon 2020 program, contributing to the development of innovative measurement techniques and virtual sensors. He has authored multiple publications in international journals and conferences and holds several patents related to measurement systems.
